About me
I am a postoctoral research fellow financed by the Italian Institute of High Mathematics (INdAM), and hosted by the research unit of Politecnico di Torino, in Italy.
I work in the field of Numerical Analysis and on its application to biological, medical and earth science modeling.
My main research interests include mixed-dimensional problems, numerical optimization, immersed problems, and error estimation.

When you don't see me doing some maths you will probably find me on my balcony chatting with my flowers or hiking around the world with my backpack!
